BOUNCE TOGETHER

A non-governmental organization established to inspire youths through Basketball to envision a better life and society, and to empower them to achieve their dreams We selflessly serve our communities and the less privileged through charitable projects designed to improve their quality of life and livelihood. This is an non-profit organisation that wants to create opportunities for young people to reach their dreams. S.E.A summer basketball camp is a project that we invision to be held annually. If you are interested to work with us or help in any way contact us! We can only do this together

Our Values

We nurture and grow the youth to become exemplary leaders in their families, careers and communities.

Goals

We inspire and empower the youth to become better leaders through education, empowerment and advocacy.

  • Give the kids the opportunity to learn skills in basketball and learn how to develop as individuals and cooperate in a group.
  • Give the kids 2 meals a day. In a previous camp, held by Sam, there was not enough food to feed the kids. Consequences were some exhausted kids that couldn’t go through the camp. One of the kids came up to Sam and told him he had to quit because he hadn’t eaten for 2 days. We want to give the kids 2 meals a day. We want to prioritize their health.
  • Give new clothes and shoes to as many kids as possible. Most of the kids last camp didn’t have functional shoes. You could see the toes sticking out on some of the kids shoes.
  • Give the opportunity of accommodation for the kids who travel far to camp and cannot go home between camp days.

Vision

Last year we had 320 kids at camp, and it cost us 300 000 SEK. We think it will cost even more next camp. We want to take in even more kids and give them more. Therefore we are searching for economical support. The reason why we believe next camp will be more expensive is because we want to better the quality by, for example, give them more shoes, balls and clothes. We will also need capital for thing outside the court, such as accommodation and food. Last year we handed out two scholarships to one girl and one boy who showed hard work and dedication in camp. These scholarships payed for their school for a whole year.
