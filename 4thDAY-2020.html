21/07/19 BIG DAY TOMORROW!

S.E.A Summer Basketball Camp is only one day away!

We met a lot of people that we are working with today. It was amazing how welcoming everyone was and how a lot of people were supporting and working to make this a successful camp.

First thing we did, as it is Sunday, was to go to church. We went to a mass held by the pastor that are letting us use the court. The picture below shows how they in the end of the service promoted the camp.

The court that we will use tomorrow is located right by the church so we got the opportunity to see it. As you can see at the pictures below the courts are not in great shape, but we got to use what we got and that is the court teams play on here in Abuja. With that said, there is beauty and culture in the hard work that is put on this court, the Gilali Basketball Court. We talked to some people telling us how they are proud developing their game on this court.

We also got the chance to see the other basketball court, where we also met the coaches that will work the camp. A lot of veteran players played pickup and the court turned out to be a hangout spot where all generations gathered to play and watch some basketball.

We came back to the Gillali basketball court later that evening and the last preparations were in action. Itâ€™s the day before the camp and there are people that are staying up late to paint the court! Together we are making this happen!

Tomorrow is gonna be the first day of the camp and everyone that we have met are excited for this to go down in Abuja!!
